Visualisations for India Water Portal

This is a collection of maps and charts created for the the India Water Portal.


Visualisations:

The collections consists of four types of charts and maps:


  • Drought Maps Rainfall data collected by the India Meteorological Department (IMD) during the hundred years period of 1905 to 1997 are used to generate these district level maps of annual incidents of drought in India.

  • Ground Water Charts: Plotting district-wise ground water levels for each state, for the period 2005-2009. The data is sourced from IMD.

  • Kausani Rainfall Charts: These charts compare monthly rainfall data for the district of Almora (Uttarkhand) collected by IMD, and daily rainfall data collected for Kausani, in the district of Almora, by Mr. David Hopkins.

  • Rainfall Maps: Map of average monthly rainfall in each district for all years from 1901 to 2002. The data is sourced from IMD.
